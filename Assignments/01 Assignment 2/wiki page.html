Opal Sankofa

  • My skincare line

    • Created in 2016 for my children suffering with eczema. I use natural and organic ingredients, Shea Butter from Ghana, organic pressed coconut oil and organic avocado oil. I also make a medicated version with CBD.

    Whipped shea butter is great all year round to protect you from the elements. In the summer cintronella oil can be infused to repel mosquitos. In the fall and winter seasons I like to add a warm vanilla and pumpkin spice scent.

    Today it is made to order, but I have hopes of being available in stores very soon.

  • Pop Ups

    • I'm currently looking for farmer markets to host a booth. I found a CBD smoke shop where I can sell my medicated shea butter.
